About the founder and leader

Apostle Oduma Akpobasa is the founder and President in council of Akpobasa Global Ministries with its Headquarters located in the Metropolitan city of Toronto, Canada. In 2011, Apostle Odum was commissioned by the Holy Ghost to establish Akpobasa Global Ministries in Toronto, Canada. Since then, they have been carrying out this mandate through the preaching and teaching of faith for the liberation and blessings of humanity!

Apostle Oduma ministers under a strong prophetic anointing; thousands have testified to being healed from cancers and other serious diseases, countless have received various miracles as the power of God is demonstrated through his son. He is a seasoned speaker at many seminars and conventions both locally and internationally. He is blissfully married to Pastor E.Akpobasa for over 25 years and they are blessed with four children.
